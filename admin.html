Iced Spanish Latte

Added by: Sarah A. | Date: Oct 25, 2025
Prep: 6 min | Servings: 1 | Calories: ~200 kcal
Taste: Sweet, creamy, and balanced
Ingredients:
  • 200 ml cold milk
  • 30 ml espresso
  • 1 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 5 ice cubes
Steps:
  • 1. Brew a fresh espresso shot (about 30 ml) and let it cool slightly.
  • 2. In a glass, mix condensed milk and sugar until dissolved.
  • 3. Add ice cubes to the glass, then pour the milk mixture.
  • 4. Slowly pour the espresso over the milk for a layered effect.
  • 5. Stir gently before drinking for a smooth, sweet blend.
Tip: Use full-fat milk for the best creamy texture, and avoid over-sweetening.

Classic Cappuccino

Added by: Laila M. | Date: Oct 20, 2025
Prep: 7 min | Servings: 1 | Calories: ~150 kcal
Taste: Rich, foamy, and balanced
Ingredients:
  • 30 ml espresso
  • 100 ml steamed milk
  • Milk foam topping
  • Pinch of cocoa powder (optional)
Steps:
  • 1. Brew an espresso shot (30 ml) into a small cup.
  • 2. Steam milk to around 65°C using a frother or steamer.
  • 3. Pour steamed milk gently over espresso.
  • 4. Spoon the milk foam on top for a thick layer.
  • 5. Sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon if desired.
Tip: Use freshly ground coffee beans for a rich flavor and creamy foam.

Iced Mocha

Added by: Omar K. | Date: Oct 22, 2025
Prep: 6 min | Servings: 1 | Calories: ~220 kcal
Taste: Chocolatey, smooth, and refreshing
Ingredients:
  • 1.5–2 oz espresso or strong coffee
  • 200 ml cold milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: whipped cream or cocoa powder for garnish
Steps:
  • 1. Brew 45–60 ml espresso, let it cool slightly for 1–2 minutes.
  • 2. Mix espresso with chocolate syrup in a tall glass until smooth.
  • 3. Add ice cubes to fill about ¾ of the glass.
  • 4. Pour cold milk over the espresso-chocolate mixture and stir gently.
  • 5. Top with whipped cream or dust with cocoa for a café-style finish.
Tip: Use dark chocolate syrup for deeper flavor, and full-fat milk for a richer texture.
